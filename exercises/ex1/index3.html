The Big Bang Theory



The Big Bang Theory is a comedy about brilliant physicists working at Cal Tech, Leonard and Sheldon, that understand how the universe works,
But none of that genius helps them interact with people, especially women. All this begins to change when beauty named Penny moves in next door.
